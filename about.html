Company

Fear is a project founded by twin brothers who have operated a game media company for 20 years in the industry acting as a game publisher, advertiser and developer. Jonathan and Patrick Carey, Co Founders of Fear, have also been developing successful casual games for the past 7 years which have attracted over 300 million viral viewers on Youtube and 350 million game plays.

Netcreeper Media Limited own the Fear project and continue to fund it's growth and development today having established it May 2021 with the assistance of their main part Dao Maker. Fear is being developed to be the only horror focused entertainment and distribution platform for games and other forms of horror media.
