TWO TOKENS ONE PORTAL TO HELL

FEAR is building an independent, censor resistant horror platform for both our own in-house game dev team and for independent horror game developers and movie makers.

The protocol is powered by both the FEAR token and the internal BLOOD token. BLOOD is an internal, illiquid in game economy token. The only way to purchase BLOOD is via the FEAR token. Once acquired, BLOOD cannot be sold but can be used to purchase all FEAR content.

BLOOD can only be acquired through FEAR and cannot be sold only traded with FEAR digital content.

FEAR TOKEN UTILITY IN OUR ECOSYSTEM

  • Acquiring Blood

    FEAR is used to purchase Blood, an illiquid in game economy token. Blood cannot be sold for USD. But can be used for content purchasing.

  • Content Purchasing

    All content in both our Horror Hub and Web Portal requires the Blood token including…

    • Games
    • Animations
    • Films
    • In Game Assets

    This offers a compelling deflationary case for the FEAR token especially given our track record of producing viral brands and games.

  • Staking

    You can stake with our token in one of our staking farms to earn an attractive yield, gain access to unique and exclusive in game assets such as skins and weapons

  • Earning

    Our games work with sustainable earning models ensuring a noninflationary outcome for our token. Using an achieve to earn economy where gameplay offers the strongest value capture followed afterwards by earning in both token and in-game assets ensures a balanced in-game economy.

  • Advertising and Product Placement

    Some of our games and animations will offer the opportunity for product placement where appropriate. If a partner wishes to advertise with us the cost will be paid in the FEAR token.

  • Advertisement Removal

    For free games remove ads by purchasing an ad free version using the FEAR token.

FEAR TOKEN VALUE FROM OUR ECOSYSTEM

  • Content Purchasing

    All FEAR tokens used in purchasing Blood for our digital content make their way into a FEAR DAO Treasury where the community can vote on token burns, staking rewards etc…

  • Staking

    Staking for in game assets requires a locking period, early exiting from this period carries a fee on the principal stake.

  • Earning

    As part of earning NFTs a small amount of the Blood token is required in the minting process. Significant when incorporated as part of a popular game where the in-game asset catalog is large.

FEAR REVENUE STREAMS

  • Mobile Purchases

    All our mobile games are offered in two forms. Blockchain enabled and fiat enabled. For fiat purchases of our games and in app purchases funds will be used to both sustain the team and also for open market buybacks of the token.

  • In Game Advertising

    Some of our games in both the Horror Hub and on Mobile will be offered free of charge but will incorporate in game advertising.

  • Game Asset Secondary Market

    A 5% fee is charged on all secondary market trades for our in-game assets.
