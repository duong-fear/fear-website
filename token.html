DUAL TOKEN SYSTEM

FEAR is building an Independent, Censor-Resistant Horror Platform for both our own in-house game dev team and for independent horror game developers and filmmakers

The protocol is powered by both the FEAR Token and the BLOOD Token.

FEAR TOKEN

Max Supply: 65,000,000 Circ Supply: Price: Market Cap:
  • The FEAR token allows consumers to purchase games, films, animations, premium NFTs and our loyalty token BLOOD in the horror ecosystem. All purchases made using the FEAR token are sent to the FEAR DAO Treasury where FEAR holders vote on token burns, sell-offs, staking rewards, or continual lock.
  • The FEAR token can only be earnt through sustainable in-game mechanisms such as leaderboards, esports, and lucky drops. All farming-type mechanisms are only rewarded in BLOOD.
  • This will lead to the deflation of the available supply of FEAR and therefore a smaller circulating supply and a scarcer token.
  • FEAR will offer a staking farm for the token where you can earn an attractive yield but more importantly stakers will be rewarded with a portion of the FEAR DAO Treasury annually.
  • Product placement and advertising partners will pay the ecosystem with FEAR tokens.
  • Free entertainment may include advertisements, these can only be removed by spending FEAR.

BLOOD TOKEN

  • BLOOD will act as the platform's loyalty token, which can be spent globally throughout the FEAR ecosystem. It is illiquid, meaning it cannot be bought and sold on a public market.
  • BLOOD can be earnt through consistently achieving goals within the ecosystem, such as daily missions in various games.
  • BLOOD can be purchased with the FEAR token via our platform.
  • Once acquired BLOOD can be used to purchase in game and film digital assets.

TOKEN FLOW

  • ALL Fees from Digital Content purchases on the FEAR platform require the FEAR Token
  • FEAR used in purchases goes to the FEAR DAO Treasury
  • Earnings in FEAR games are sustainable and non-inflatinoary

REVENUE FLOW

  • STAKING Fees from early exits go to the FEAR DAO Treasury
  • Staking rewards provide a low-inflation yield topped up by a Real Yield from our Digital content fees.
  • A Percentage of Revenue from Mobile and Desktop sales is used to buy back the token.
