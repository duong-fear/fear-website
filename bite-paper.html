THE PROPHECY OF FEAR

FEAR is Evolving from a Play to Earn Blockchain based project into a Horror Digital Content Platform serving our large community of Horror Fans.

STRATEGY

What does this mean for the Token? In order to consume any of our content the token will be used for purchasing on both desktop and mobile. All it takes is for one of our future games, films or animations to go viral to drive extreme demand in the token.

Earnings in any of our content are driven from demand in the token, meaning earnings are not offered passively for gamers outside of our low inflation staking farms.

We are building a platform that is both retail friendly and requires no crypto knowledge required which assists in offering a strong conversion funnel for potential new horror fans wishing to join our community. A larger community means more token demand

DEFLATIONARY FUTURE

FEAR will be required for all Digital content purchases ranging from Games, NFTs, Animations all the way through to Films generating fees that flow into a project treasury where annual burns and rewards to stakers will be issued.

The key take home? FEAR is on its way to becoming a deflationary token but crucially one driven by real world demand.
